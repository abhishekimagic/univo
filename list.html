The Multimedia Editor should be able to create, edit and assemble visual elements and videos raw material into a suitable, finished product as per standards and guidelines so that it is ready for uploading and delivery to students. S/he will work closely with program managers, faculty and all verticals in the content production team to ensure that the multimedia output is consistent and meets the quality parameters.

Responsibilities:

  • Conceptualize and develop visual elements based on requirements
  • Record/create and edit videos and audios to meet course content requirements
  • Create and edit high-end graphics as per defined standards
  • Edit and Improve aesthetics of Powerpoint slides based on requirements
  • Develop illustrations, logos and other designs
  • Assist in the development of creative approaches to visual elements that will enhance student engagement
  • Maintain working knowledge of new technology, equipment, systems and software pertaining to the studio, post-production, videos and graphics
  • Researching and submitting requests for software and equipment upgrades that will enhance and improve the production and post production workflow of the studio and post production facility
  • Follow project plan and timelines of content development, uploading and updating process.
  • Assimilate feedback quickly and covert it to actionable
  • Co-ordinate with cross functional teams
  • Support the team and guide for project specific queries and solutions.

Skills and Requirements:

  • Excellent knowledge of Graphics and Video for online learning
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Flexibility to meet changing deadlines
  • Resourcefulness to quickly learn and apply new skills readily
  • Technical aptitude (i.e., learn new applications quickly)
  • Strong teamwork ethic (works amicably with others)
  • Ability to interpret feedback on content and convert them into actionable
  • Ability to provide clear and concise feedback
  • Good communication skills
  • Knowledge of layouts, graphic fundamentals, typography and print, well versed with design software (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop , Ms Powerpoint)
  • Knowledge of video editing software (Adobe premiere pro , Adobe after effects)
